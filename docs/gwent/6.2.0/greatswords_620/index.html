Description

Skellige deck that relies on An Craite Greatswords and Dagur getting boosted when the opponent's units get damaged. In combination with Morkvarg and Wild Boar of the Sea this can be used for a big swing the last turn.

This is one of the Decks that got me to Pro Rank for the first time (Season of the Viper, 2020). Also got to Pro during the Season of Magic (2020), where it did well on Pro Ladder.
