Description

This deck allowed me to climb up fast to Pro Rank (in combination with Skellige), though typically greedy strategies don't work well on Pro Ladder, as a matter of fact, the abundance of control decks and tall punish in the meta made Syndicate a poor option. Ultimately, I abandoned this faction in favor of Northern Realms.
