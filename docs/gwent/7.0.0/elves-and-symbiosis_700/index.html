Description

Elves with Symbiosis list I played in Pro Rank during the Season of the Griffin in 2020. Most of the times you'll want to spend a lot to win the first round and push hard the second round while keeping one of your bombs for the last round. It is not an easy deck to play and with no way to deal with tall units it can struggle in certain matchups.
